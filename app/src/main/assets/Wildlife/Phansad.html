Phansad Wildlife Sanctuary is a wildlife sanctuary in the Murud and Roha talukas of Raigad district, Maharashtra state, India. Spread over an area of 52 sq. km, the Phansad bird sanctuary is another beautiful wildlife sanctuaries in Maharashtra that you must visit. This is home to many mammals and other animals.Phansad Wildlife Sanctuary is also known as Phansad Bird Sanctuary.

The Phansad sanctuary is home to over 279 different bird species of fauna and 720 species of flora. The bird sanctuary which is located near Kashid Beach is another attraction for the tourists. One can relax on the beach after watching the beautiful birds in the sanctuary.

Best time to visit: The best time to visit Phansad bird Sanctuary is between November and March.

Location: Raigad district, Maharashtra.

Safari timings: The time to visit the sanctuary is between 8 am to 8 pm.
