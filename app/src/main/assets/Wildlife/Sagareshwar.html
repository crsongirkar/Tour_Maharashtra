Sagareshwar Wildlife Sanctuary is a manmade sanctuary that is in the state of Maharashtra. The plants in the Sanctuary is all planted by a human being and different animal species are induced in the sanctuary and it is one of the best wildlife sanctuaries in Maharashtra. Sagareshwar Wildlife Sanctuary is a protected area in the Indian state of Maharashtra.

Animals like wild boars, blackbuck, sambar, deer are also being found in abundance in the manmade sanctuary. Reptiles, insects and different other birds have made a home in this sanctuary.

The wildlife sanctuary is man-made; it is an artificially cultivated forest without a perennial supply of water, and most of the wildlife species were artificially introduced.

Best time to visit: The best time to visit the Sanctuary is from August to February.

Location: Sangli district, Maharashtra.

Safari timings: One can visit the Sanctuary during the day time.
