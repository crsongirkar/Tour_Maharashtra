Another popular Maharashtra wildlife sanctuaries that tourist must explore during their visit in Maharashtra is Gautala wildlife sanctuary. also known as Gautala Autramghat Sanctuary. The sanctuary is named after its neighboring village Gautala and is a beautiful shelter for plenty of animals, reptiles and birds alike.

The sanctuary is an abode for a number of Animals species including big cats and primates. Around 230 separate species of birds can be spotted in the region. Reptiles like Cobra, Krait, Keelback Viper, Python, Rat snake, Monitor Lizard found here.

Best time to visit: The best time to visit Gautala wildlife sanctuary is between the months January to March.

Location: Aurangabad District, Maharashtra.

Safari timings: The safari timings are between 9 am to 5 pm
