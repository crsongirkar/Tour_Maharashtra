Karnala Bird Sanctuary is one of the most famous destinations for people who like hiking and watching birds. Located in Panvel Taluka of Raigad District, near Mumbai city, the place attracts many people. Along with the 222 residential spices of birds the sanctuary also attracts many migratory birds and it is one of the famous wildlife sanctuaries in Maharashtra.

Nilgiri Wood pigeon, woodpeckers, Malabar Grey Hornbill, Green Pigeon are some of the famous birds in the sanctuary. During the winter, the sanctuary also attracts migratory birds. Bird view towers, Karnala Fort are some of the added places which one can visit in the sanctuary.

Best time to visit: For residential birds, Monsoon is the best time to visit and for migratory birds, one can visit the sanctuary during the winters.

Location: Located at Panvel Taluka of Maharashtra State.

Safari timings: 9 am to 6pm is the ideal time to visit the sanctuary.
