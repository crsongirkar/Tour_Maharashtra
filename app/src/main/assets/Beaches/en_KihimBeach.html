Kihim beach is one of the best known beaches in and around Alibaug. It is famous for its dense cover of Coconut trees. It is a wonderful beach with the perfect combination of beauty and development, solitude and activity. This place is well known for variety of woods which acts as a cover. Nature lovers shall enjoy visiting this place as it provides sight of rare butterflies, birds and flowers.

Kihim beach lies on the Konkan coast of Maharashtra, just about 100 kms from Mumbai and 10kms from Alibag.It is the best place to experience the enchanting view of sunset from the sandy beach. The major attraction of Kihim is the sandy beach itself. People go to the beach to watch those rare species of birds, which is why it also known as the ‘Bird Watchers’ Paradise’.

Best time to visit is between October and March, when the weather is moderate and cool.
