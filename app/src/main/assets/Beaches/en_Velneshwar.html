The Velneshwar Beach Located close to Velneshwar village in Maharashtra, which is to the North of Shastri River, Velneshwar beach lined with coconut trees is popular for its rock-less sea and clean atmosphere. Whether you want to laze around on the beach, sunbathe, or take a swim, you can do all that here. You can even spend hours on end simply taking in the natural beauty of the place and its clean environs.
