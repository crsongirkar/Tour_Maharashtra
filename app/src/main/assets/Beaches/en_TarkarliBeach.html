Tarkarli beach near Malvan is a versatile destination that brings you the best of some of the most sought-after activities across popular tourist destinations in the country in an undisturbed setting.

Tarkarli Beach is a narrow stretch of beach which is situated at the convergence of the Karli River and the Arabian Sea. This beach is famous because of its transparent clear water. Tarkarli Beach is also known as ‘Queen Beach’ of Sindhudurg. One can experience snorkelling and scuba diving at Tarkarli Beach. Taste the special Malvani food such as variety of fish items, ‘kombadi-vada’ during your visit to Tarkarli.

The popular banana boat rides and parasailing of Goa, the trending tourist attraction of spotting dolphins, the houseboats ride on backwaters of Kerala, the sought-after snorkeling, and scuba diving experiences of Andaman’s, this place has got it all.

The beauty of a getaway at Tarkarli is enhanced manifold by the unique landscape around it.

  • Deobagh, an outgrown village of fishermen, also known as God’s garden. You can rent their boats for a brief sea tour.
  • Dhamapur Lake best known for its calm, still water, and a pervading silence ideal for some soul-searching.
  • Shree Shiv Chhatrapati Temple dedicated to iconic Maratha warrior Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, built by his son Chhatrapati Rajaram Maharaj.
  • Padmagarh Fort, another great structure on the Konkan coastline that is sure to leave you impressed.
  • Tsunami island
  • Karli river
