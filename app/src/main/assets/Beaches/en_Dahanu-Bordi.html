Dahanu is a coastal city situated in Palghar District. The prime tourist attraction in Dahanu is Dahanu-Bordi Beach. This famous beach is situated about 145 km away from Mumbai. It is a 17 km stretch along the sea coast. The Dahanu-Bordi beach is famous for chikoo. Bordi is half-hours drive away from Dahanu. Although, it is quite warm during the summers but, the gentle breeze cools down the entire beach.

The natural and calming effect of Bordi Dahanu together with its dark, black colored sand attracts lots of tourists from far and wide. This beach is located at Bordi – a small village, which is famous for various educational institutions and boarding schools. The beach itself is the major attraction here because visitors can either take a stroll or simply laze around to have a relaxing time.

You may enjoy an overnight picnic here as well since Bordi Dahanu beach offers a camping ground. If you are a fruit lover, you can treat your taste buds to some of the best Chikoo (Sapodilla) of India that’s available here.

Winter (between November and February) is the ideal time to visit Bordi Dahanu beach.
