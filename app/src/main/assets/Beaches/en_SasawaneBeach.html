Sasawane Beach belongs to the little village of Sasawane, which is part of Alibag Taluka in Maharashtra. It is one of the least explored beaches and this is exclusively calm to enjoy a relaxing holiday. It is about 20 kms from Alibaug city.

Apart from Sasawane Beach with its scenic skyline view, the Karmarkar Sculpture Museum is the major attraction here. People also visit the Shree Fulai Mata Mandir in Sasawane. Alibaug is the nearest place filled with multitude of forts, temples and beaches.

Best Time to Visit, winter is the best season, though you can plan your trip anytime between the months of October and early April.
