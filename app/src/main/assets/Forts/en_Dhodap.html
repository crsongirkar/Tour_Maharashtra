Dhodap fort is a mountain top fort situated in Kalwan taluka in Nashik of Maharashtra. It is a famous tourist attraction in this site and the second highest mountain fort in the Sahyadri ranges. It is the third highest peak in Maharashtra. The upper section of the fort is built with brick and stone.

Unknown to many, the fort offers great trekking experience. It is a joy and a trekker’s paradise. There is an idol of Hanuman inside the fort and there are caves at the base of the fort. The unique features on the trek and the arched doorway take you back in time.

According to historical sources, this fort played an important role during the Peshwa period. The historical battle of Dindori was fought in this fort.

If you are planning for an adventure Maharashtra tour, this is a good destination for you. Apart from the fort there are other places to visit like Shkembi cliff and Ikhara cliff. The views from Dhodap are spectacular. Being the second highest fort, you can get a bird’s eye view of the entire region.

How to Reach: The Dhodap Fort Trek starts from a small village called Hatti in the Nashik district of the Maharashtra state.
Hatti village is about 60 km from Nashik.
