Maharashtra, a popular destination in India for tourist. Its blessed with spectacular scenic beauty, a dreamy coastline and vast expanse of greenery, is ideal for enjoying Hot Air Balloon safari ride. Nothing can be more adventurous than flying in the air and drifting away with the wind’s direction.

If you’re looking for an off-beat activity, head over to try hot air balloon rides in Lonavala. Hot air ballooning is now the latest craze among those willing to try out something entirely new and refreshing.

Hot air ballooning in Lonavala :

Lonavala is one of the most beautiful and picturesque hill stations in Maharashtra. The hot air balloon ride in Lonavala will give you splendid views of the lush-green fields, mist-covered lakes, tumbling waterfalls, ruins of forts, and undulating valleys of the Sahyadris. This hot air balloon ride is the perfect escape from the bustling city life.

Making beautiful memories with your loved ones! You will get to enjoy 360 degree views of the scenic Sahyadri valley, hills and locales in Lonavala, while flying 4000 feet high in the air!

The ideal time of the day to enjoy a hot air balloon safari ride in Lonavala is either before sunrise or just before the sunset. Starting from the month of September till the end of March, can be the best time to relish this exhilarating adventure activities in Maharashtra. During this time, the weather remains dry and is not really hot. Hot air ballooning in Lonavala is sure to lend you with an experience of a lifetime.
