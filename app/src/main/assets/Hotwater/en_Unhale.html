Rajapur Hot Water Spring, also known as Unhale Hot Water Spring is situated in the village of Unhale near the city of Rajapur. This hot water spring is on the floodplain of Kondavi river. It is the southern-most hot water spring along India’s west coast.

Hot water of 42°C temperature flows through the outlet provided in the bath facility. The hot water of this spring is rich with sulfur and is famous for curing skin diseases. This place located 72km from Ratnagiri.

Unlike other spring, there isn’t a pool at Rajapur. Instead hot spring water temperature flows through the tap inside the bathing facility. There are two separate bathing units for women and men, respectively.
