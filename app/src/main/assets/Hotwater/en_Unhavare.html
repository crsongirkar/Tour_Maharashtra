Hot water springs can be all around the Konkan region, in the districts of Thane, Raigad, Ratnagiri, etc. Many tourists crowd these places often for spiritual or leisure purposes. The hot water spring is at a village named Unhavare. This village is based at 35 Kms away from the city of Dapoli.

This pool is perennial, there is hot water here all round the year. The water level remains constant during winters and monsoon as well. Even the temperature of the water never changes and the temperature of water is 70 degrees Celsius. These waters contain a mixture of Sulphur and Carbon dioxide. As the amount of Sulphur is higher, these waters can cure skin diseases and are a perfect solution for stress. There is even an enclosed and private bath pond for women. Having a bath in these waters is a relaxing experience.

Location: Unhavare (Dapoli), Maharashtra.
