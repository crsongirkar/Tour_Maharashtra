Jummapatti is a small station in Maharashtra famous among nature lovers for housing one of the most scenic waterfalls in Lonavala. The station lies on Neral – Matheran toy train route and is blessed with thick foliage everywhere you look.

Apart from spending some quiet moments by the waterfall soaking in its beauty, you can also indulge in nature walk experiences that are really rewarding. The sight created by the white stream cascading on grey rocks in the middle of greens of the forest is something you would like to look at from up close.

Height: 120 Feet
Location: Jummapatti, Neral – Matheran Train Route
How to reach: Get off at Jummapatti train station and walk up to the waterfall.
