Get away from the concrete jungles awhile and take a breath of fresh air at Bhivpuri Waterfall, an excellent nature retreat in Lonavala. Plummeting from a height of approximately 20 feet in a rainy season, the waterfall appears magical.

However, more than its natural splendor the waterfall is famous for the wealth of experiences one can indulge in over here. If you would love to try a thrilling waterfall rappelling experience, visit this waterfall and let the daredevil in you be amazed. There are also some exciting jungle trails near the waterfall that offer some fun time to trekkers coming here.

Height: 20 feet
Location: Karjat, Lonavala
How to reach: Take an autorickshaw to reach the village from where the waterfall is a 20-minute walk.
