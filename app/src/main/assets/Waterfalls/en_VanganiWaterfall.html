Vangani Waterfall is one of the most famous historic waterfalls, and is situated in the Bedisgaon village of Ambernath Taluka. The waterfall is quite close to Vangani town. It is a serene village with River Ulhas flowing on one side and chain of small hillocks on the other.

Vangani enjoys heavy rainfall during monsoon, and it leaves the entire region green and pictorially beautiful with mesmerizing views. Vangani Waterfall is as beautiful and safe destination.

This waterfall is also locally known as Bhagirath Waterfalls, It is a must visit place for couples and adventure lovers because of its peaceful and romantic location.You can also try rock climbing here

Height : 130 Feet

Location : Bedisgaon, Maharashtra

How to reach:

  • The waterfall is simply 2 km from Vangani railway station
  • By road Vangani can be reached through the Panvel-Karjat route or through Airoli-Shilphata-Badlapur MIDC route.
